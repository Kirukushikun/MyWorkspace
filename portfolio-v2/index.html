Background
ABOUT
ME

Graduate of Dominican College of Tarlac with a Bachelor's in Information Technology, and a KodeGo full stack bootcamp alum. Shortly after graduation, I joined Brookside Group of Companies as their sole web developer.

Nine production systems shipped in the span of a year, each going through the full cycle: Figma prototype, UI mockups, development, and live deployment. I manage five hosting environments, navigate servers via SSH, configure Docker containers, and handle DNS through Cloudflare. The stack grew to match the work — Laravel, Livewire, Alpine, Tailwind, React, Inertia, and AI integrations through OpenRouter and Google APIs.

Outside company work, I build personal browser tools — CardForge, ClipForge, LiftLens, ICStudio — an ongoing experiment in focused, zero-dependency product design.

I don't just write code. I own the whole thing.
